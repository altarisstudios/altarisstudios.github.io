Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy for the mobile game Chromatically.

Last updated: July 4, 2026

1. Overview

Altaris Studio respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the mobile game Chromatically handles user and device information.

2. Information We Collect

Chromatically does not intentionally collect personal information from users.

The game does not request or store information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Precise location
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media files
  • Financial information

3. Local Game Data

Chromatically may store game progress, records, settings, and similar gameplay information locally on your device. This data remains on your device and is not sent to Altaris Studio servers.

4. Internet and Online Services

The current version of Chromatically does not require account login and does not use online user accounts. Some platform services, such as Google Play, may process technical information according to their own policies.

5. Advertising and In-App Purchases

The current version of Chromatically does not display third-party advertisements and does not include in-app purchases. If advertising or purchases are introduced in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Sharing

Altaris Studio does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Chromatically is designed as a casual puzzle game for a general audience. Altaris Studio does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Data Security

Since Chromatically does not intentionally collect personal information, no personal user database is maintained by Altaris Studio for this game.

9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page.
